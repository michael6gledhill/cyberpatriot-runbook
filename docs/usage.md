# Usage

- Configure settings (server IP, DB name, credentials) from Settings page (placeholder in dashboard).
- Sign up with role: admin, coach, team captain, mentor, competitor.
- Login; pending accounts require approval as per rules.
- Create teams (admin/coach); coaches auto-added.
- Create checklists; items show name/description initially; click to expand steps and mark status.
- Readmes are team-visible; forensic questions can be asked and answered by team members.
- Notes are private per user; coaches/mentors/captains may view within team.
